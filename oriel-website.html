About ORiEL

Where light finds its place.

ORiEL is a home-grown collection of living plants, thoughtful décor, and quiet details curated for everyday spaces.

Inspired by the way light rests in corners, on windowsills, and across still rooms, ORiEL brings together plants in glass, rooted forms, natural textures, and artful accents that belong — not as decoration, but as part of living.

Each piece is chosen or created with care, designed to settle gently into your home. Along with every plant comes simple guidance, nurturing essentials, and an invitation to grow it with ease.

ORiEL is not a store.

It is a lived space — one where light, life, and home meet naturally.
